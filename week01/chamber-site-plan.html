Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to inform existing and potential clients of our business offers, allow them to contact us directly with inquiries and feedback, and make online bookings with us.

Audience


Who are the target customers?

Our main customers are students and young professionals. They like spending weekends and holidays outdoors hiking, kayaking, rafting, cycling and etc.

What do they want?

They want to spend time actively engaged in sports with their peers.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

They expect to find current offers, schedule, pricing and terms for our water rafting services, and make instant bookings.
